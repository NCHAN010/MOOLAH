Primary Component

Primary Components comprise of long term goals such as housing, cars and retirement plans. You will be asked what is the goal date that you want to work towards to. At the end of every month, you can decide how much money you want to put in. There will be a progress bar showing how near or far you are towards that goal.

Constant Component

Constant Components are fixed amounts of money that will be added or deducted accordingly. For example, you can add your salary as a positive constant component that is incremeneted monthly. We can add things like existing house/car mortgages, insurance plans as negative constant components as they are deducted every month in fixed amounts as well.(I am currently working on a shceduler application that is not available at the moment.

Secondary Component

Secondary Components are short term goals of small earnings or spendings every month. Users can use this to track how much money they spend on food, clothes , makeup as negative secondary components. Users can add one time positive increments like pay bonuses as a postive secondary components as well.

AIM:

The aim of this website is to help users gauge and keep track of short term and long term life goals.
